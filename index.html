about us


Horse riding tours in Kyrgyzstan As a part of horseback riding tours in Kyrgyzstan, you will follow the routes of nomads, touch their life and culture, and visit picturesque places. You will be able to live in kyrgyz yurts, taste local food and kumis(mare's milk).


Ski tours to Kyrgyzstan Kyrgyzstan is a country of mountains. And where there are mountains, there is always a place for skiing and snowboarding. We offer you excellent ski tours to the best resorts of Karakol and Suusamyr, as well as the opportunity to participate in heli-skiing
